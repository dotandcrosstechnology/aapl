Mathematical Framework

Step 1: Tree Construction

Time is divided into $N$ equal intervals[cite: 35]. Prices move up ($u$) or down ($d$):

$u = e^{\sigma\sqrt{\Delta t}}$, $d = 1/u$ [cite: 37]

Step 2: Risk-Neutral Measure

Probability of an up-move ($p$):

$p = \frac{e^{r\Delta t} - d}{u - d}$ [cite: 39]
